About Me

I'm Tommy, a second year student studying at The University of Waterloo and I'm studying Global Business and Digital Arts. I've been passionate about design ever since I saw the old Porsche advertisements from the 90s when I was 10. Design is something I love because it allows me to develop and share my own thoughts and creativity to the world. You could call it my paradise.

My brother and my mom both made me feel like I can be myself when i was little. They both were and still are my biggest inspirations in design under Eero Saarinen and Louis Cartier. They gave me the confidence as a kid to share with the world who I was, to keep designing and following my path.
