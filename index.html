About Me

I'm Tommy, a second year University of Waterloo student and I'm studying Global Business and Digital Arts. I've been passionate about design ever since I saw the old Porsche advertisements from the 90s when I was 10. Design is something I love because it allows me to develop and share my own thoughts and views. You could call it my paradise.

My brother and my mom both make me feel like I can be myself. They both were and are my biggest inspirations in design under Eero Saarinen and Louis Cartier. They gave me the confidence as a kid to share with the world who I was. To keep designing and following my path.
